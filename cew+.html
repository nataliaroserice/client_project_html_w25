Center for the Education of Women+ (CEW+)

About CEW+

CEW+ supports the professional, educational, and personal development of students and community members, especially those in underserved populations. The Center's programs empower individuals to achieve their goals by addressing the unique challenges they face.

Services Offered

  • Scholarships and Fellowships: Financial support designed to help students overcome obstacles to education. CEW+ offers several funding opportunities for students facing financial barriers. Learn more and apply.


  • Counseling Services: Career and academic counseling tailored to your needs.

  • Workshops and Events: Topics include leadership development, career planning, and financial wellness.

  • Advocacy: Initiatives and programs focused on equity, access, and inclusion.

How to Access CEW+ Services

  1. Visit the CEW+ office at 330 E. Liberty Street, Ann Arbor, MI 48104 during business hours.
  2. Schedule a counseling appointment by calling 734-764-6360 or using the online booking portal.
  3. Register for workshops and events via their Events Page.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who is eligible for CEW+ services? All students, faculty, and staff, with a focus on supporting underserved populations.
  • How can I apply for scholarships? Visit the Funding Page for more information.
  • Is there a cost for counseling? No, counseling services are free for University of Michigan students.
