Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS)

About CAPS

CAPS provides mental health services tailored to help students navigate academic and personal challenges. Their mission is to promote student wellness and success through accessible, inclusive, and high-quality care.

Services Offered

  • Individual Counseling: One-on-one support to address personal, academic, or mental health concerns.
  • Group Therapy: Join peers in sessions focused on stress management, identity exploration, or other shared challenges.
  • Wellness Workshops: Build skills for mindfulness, self-care, and handling stress effectively.
  • Crisis Support: 24/7 emergency mental health assistance for students in urgent need.

How to Access CAPS

Scheduling an appointment is simple:

  1. Make an appointment with UMSI's embedded counselor, Ashley Evearitt, Psy.D, by emailing Ashley directly at evearitt@umed.umich.edu.
  2. Visit the CAPS office in the Michigan Union (4th Floor) or call 734-764-8312.
  3. Log into the Student Portal to book online.
  4. Attend one of our drop-in hours for immediate support.

Crisis Resources

If you or someone you know is experiencing a mental health emergency, please use the following resources:

  • CAPS Urgent Support: Available in-person or via phone, Monday through Friday, 10am - 4:30pm. Call 734-764-8312.
  • CAPS Phone Urgent Support After Hours: Available 24/7: Evenings, Weekends, Holidays). Call 734-764-8312. Press 0.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is CAPS free? Yes, all services provided by CAPS are free for students.
  • How long are counseling sessions? Sessions typically last 50 minutes.
  • Can I get long-term counseling? CAPS focuses on short-term care but can provide referrals for ongoing support.
